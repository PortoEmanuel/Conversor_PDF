How to Install the Application:
=============
1. Uninstall MicroG (System Settings> Apps > microG)
2. Uninstall YouTube ReVanced
3. Disable original YouTube (System Settings > Apps > YouTube)
4. Restart phone
5. Install the new GMS-Core (MicroG) made by the ReVanced team (https://github.com/ReVanced/GmsCore/releases/latest)
6. Login on the new MicroG
7. Install preferred YouTube ReVanced release
8. Open YouTube ReVanced
9. Accept the restart prompt to apply layout
10. Clear YouTube ReVanced from recents
11. Force kill YouTube Revanced (System Settings > Apps > YouTube ReVanced)
12. Launch YouTube ReVanced and enjoy
